HTML Entities Examples

This page demonstrates the use of HTML entities to display special characters

Basic HTML Special Characters

The following characters have special meaning in HTML and must be encoded:

  • Less than: < (uses &lt;)
  • Greater than: > (uses &gt;)
  • Ampersand: & (uses &amp;)
  • Double quote: " (uses &quot;)
  • Single quote: ' (uses &apos;)

Copyright and Trademark Symbols

Common business and legal symbols:

  • Copyright: © (uses &copy;)
  • Trademark: ™ (uses &trade;)
  • Registered: ® (uses &reg;)
