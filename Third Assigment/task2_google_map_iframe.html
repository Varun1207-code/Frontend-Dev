Google Map Embed Example

This page demonstrates embedding a Google Map using iframe with lazy loading

Location: New Delhi, India

Below is an interactive Google Map showing New Delhi, the capital city of India.

About New Delhi

New Delhi is the capital of India and a part of the National Capital Territory of Delhi. It is the seat of all three branches of the Government of India, including the executive, legislative, and judiciary. The city is known for its rich history, beautiful architecture, and cultural landmarks.

Key Features:

  • Population: Over 30 million in the metropolitan area
  • Area: 1,484 square kilometers
  • Famous landmarks: Red Fort, India Gate, Lotus Temple
  • Government seat: Houses Parliament, Supreme Court, and President's residence

Technical Implementation Details

Title attribute: Provides accessible description of the map content
Width & Height: Set to 600x450 pixels for optimal viewing
Loading="lazy": Improves page performance by loading map only when needed
Allowfullscreen: Enables fullscreen map viewing
Referrerpolicy: Controls what referrer information is sent with requests

What does loading="lazy" do?

The loading="lazy" attribute is a performance optimization that:

  • Defers loading: The iframe content is not loaded immediately when the page loads
  • Loads on demand: Content loads only when the user scrolls near the iframe
  • Saves bandwidth: Reduces initial page load time and data usage
  • Improves performance: Especially beneficial for pages with multiple embedded content
  • Better UX: Users see the main page content faster
